OUIIMI PROJECT - CORE REQUIREMENTS & FUNCTIONALITIES
====================================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
ouiimi is a community-driven booking platform for everyday service-based businesses.
Goal: Simple, fair, and transparent way to take bookings for hair, nails, beauty, wellness, and pet grooming services.

BUSINESS MODEL
--------------
Payment Structure:
- Customer pays: 10% deposit + $1.99 ouiimi service fee
- Remaining 90% paid directly to business at appointment
- Business receives: 50% of the 10% deposit (5% of total service cost) after completion
- ouiimi keeps: 50% of deposit (5% of total) + $1.99 service fee

Cancellation Policy:
- User cancellation: Business receives 50% of deposit, service fee non-refundable
- Business cancellation: User receives full 10% deposit refund (service fee non-refundable)
- Refunds processed within 3-5 business days

SERVICE CATEGORIES & STRUCTURE
-------------------------------
Main Categories:
1. Hair Services (with sub-categories: Haircuts, Colouring, Blow-Dry & Styling, Treatments)
2. Nails
3. Beauty & Brows
4. Massage & Wellness
5. Skin & Facials
6. Dog Grooming (with sub-categories: Bathing, Haircuts & Styling, Coat & Skin Care, Nails & Paws, Ears/Eyes/Teeth)

Service Structure:
- Each service can have pre-programmed sub-services
- Each service can have multiple add-ons (up-sell items)
- Services can have multiple date/time slots
- Multiple staff members can be assigned to same service/time slot
- Each service has: name, duration, cost, description, address

USER TYPES
----------
1. Shoppers (Customers)
   - Can browse services
   - Can book appointments
   - Can manage bookings (upcoming, finished, cancelled)
   - Can rebook services
   - Can cancel bookings

2. Small Businesses
   - Must first create shopper account
   - Request to become seller/business
   - Approval workflow required
   - Can list services
   - Can manage staff
   - Can view/manage bookings
   - Can complete bookings
   - Can cancel bookings

3. Admin
   - Approves business requests
   - Manages payouts to businesses
   - Views all bookings

BOOKING FLOW
------------
1. Home Page Discovery
   - Shows services by category
   - First 6 cards per category with "More" button
   - Hair Services and Dog Grooming show sub-categories separately
   - "Book" button for date/location-based search

2. Service Selection
   - Click on service card to view details
   - Card shows: Business logo, name, service name, duration, cost, next available date/time

3. Booking Form
   - Select date (from available dates)
   - Select service (pre-filled if from card)
   - Select preferred staff member
   - Select time slot
   - View address (from business listing)
   - View description (from business)
   - View base cost
   - Select add-ons (optional)
   - See total cost (base + add-ons)
   - Click "Book Now" to add to cart

4. Cart & Checkout
   - Can add multiple services from same business
   - Can only checkout from one business at a time
   - Shows: Service details, cost breakdown, 10% deposit, $1.99 fee, total today
   - Payment via Stripe
   - 2-minute block-out on selected time/date during checkout

5. Booking Confirmation
   - Email sent to shopper
   - Email sent to business
   - Booking appears in "Upcoming" for both

BOOKING STATUSES
----------------
1. Upcoming: Future bookings, sorted by date/time (closest first)
2. Pending: Completed bookings waiting for business to mark as complete
3. Finished: Completed bookings
4. Cancelled: Cancelled bookings (by shopper or business)

AUTOMATIC TRANSITIONS
---------------------
- At end of day (00:00) or after booking time passes: Upcoming → Pending
- Business marks as complete: Pending → Finished
- Cancelled bookings go to Finished section with "Cancelled" label

STAFF MANAGEMENT
----------------
- Businesses can list multiple staff members
- Each staff has: name, photo, qualifications/about
- Multiple staff can be assigned to same service/time slot
- System blocks staff if already booked during selected time
- Extra 30-40 mins buffer between bookings for add-ons
- Staff availability shown in dropdown (busy staff marked)

SERVICE LISTING (Business)
---------------------------
- Business selects category (dropdown)
- If category has sub-categories: shows sub-category list, then service selection
- If no sub-categories: direct service selection
- Select service
- Add date via calendar
- For each date: add time slots, cost, assign staff
- Can add multiple dates
- Can add add-ons with custom pricing
- Add description
- Set address (where service takes place)
- List service

TIME SLOT MANAGEMENT
--------------------
- Calendar picker for date selection
- For each date: can add multiple time slots
- Each time slot: start time, end time, cost, staff assignment
- Multiple staff can be assigned to same slot
- System automatically deletes past dates/time slots
- Time slots delete at their start time if not booked

DOUBLE BOOKING PREVENTION
-------------------------
- Staff members blocked if already booked during selected time
- 2-minute block-out during checkout process
- Extra 30-40 mins buffer between bookings for add-ons
- System prevents same time slot from being booked twice

SHOPPER PROFILE FEATURES
------------------------
- Bookings Tab: Shows upcoming bookings sorted by date/time
- Calendar view: Click date to see bookings for that day
- Finished Bookings: Completed and cancelled bookings
- Details Tab: Edit name, email, phone number
- Contact: View admin and business contact info
- Rebook: Redirects to business profile to book again
- Cancel: Cancels booking (loses deposit + fee)

BUSINESS PROFILE FEATURES
-------------------------
- Story Tab: Business story/description
- Services Tab: All listed services, can filter by date via calendar
- Staff Tab: List of staff members, click to see details
- Bookings Tab: 
  - Upcoming: Future bookings
  - Pending: Completed, waiting for business to mark complete
  - Finished: Completed bookings
  - Can filter by date, category, sub-category
- List Tab: All listed but not booked services
- Details Tab: Edit business info, bank details

BUSINESS PAYMENT PROCESS
------------------------
- After business marks booking as "complete"
- Business enters bank details: Name, BSB, Account Number, Contact Number
- ouiimi pays: 50% of 10% deposit (5% of total service cost)
- Payment processed within 1-2 business days
- Admin panel tracks: Booking ID, Shopper, Total 10%, Service charges (5%), Business 5%, Status, BSB, Account Number

EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS
-------------------
1. Account Verification
2. Welcome/Signup Confirmation
3. Business Welcome (pending approval)
4. Business Approval Confirmation
5. Booking Confirmation (Shopper)
6. Booking Confirmation (Business)
7. Appointment Reminder (Shopper)
8. Booking Completed (Shopper)
9. Cancellation Notification (Shopper cancels)
10. Cancellation (Business cancels - shopper receives)
11. Cancellation Confirmation (Business)
12. Payment Receipt (Business - after completion)
13. Refund Confirmation (Shopper)

HOME PAGE DESIGN
---------------
- Nav bar: Logo (links to home), Book button, Cart icon
- Sidebar: About, Shopper, Small Business, How it works (appears on all pages)
- Service categories displayed
- First 6 service cards per category
- "More" button to see all services in category
- Hair Services and Dog Grooming: sub-categories shown separately

SEARCH & FILTERING
------------------
- "Book" button: Location input (auto-suggest), Date picker (calendar)
- Category dropdown
- Sub-category dropdown (for Hair Services, Dog Grooming)
- Service dropdown
- Results filtered by location, date, category

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
----------------------
- Pre-programmed service categories and sub-categories
- Pre-programmed add-ons per category
- Calendar component for date selection
- Time slot management system
- Staff availability tracking
- Double booking prevention
- Automatic date/time slot cleanup
- Email notification system
- Payment processing (Stripe)
- Bank details collection for business payouts
- Booking status management
- Cart system (one business at a time)
