OUIIMI - USER FLOWS & INTERACTIONS
===================================

SHOPPER FLOW
------------
1. Discovery
   - Visit home page
   - Browse services by category
   - See first 6 cards per category
   - Click "More" to see all services
   - OR use "Book" button to search by location/date

2. Service Selection
   - Click on service card
   - View service details page
   - See: Business info, service name, duration, cost, next available date/time

3. Booking Process
   - Select date (from available dates)
   - Service pre-filled (if from card)
   - Select staff member (dropdown, busy staff marked)
   - Select time slot (from available slots)
   - View address (business location)
   - View description (business-provided)
   - View base cost
   - Select add-ons (optional, from dropdown)
   - See total cost (base + add-ons)
   - Click "Book Now" → Added to cart

4. Cart & Checkout
   - View cart with booking details
   - Can add more services from same business
   - See cost breakdown:
     * Service cost
     * Add-ons cost
     * Total service cost
     * 10% deposit
     * $1.99 ouiimi fee
     * Total today
   - Fill payment form (Stripe)
   - Complete payment
   - Receive confirmation email
   - Booking appears in "Upcoming"

5. Booking Management
   - View "Bookings" tab
   - See upcoming bookings (sorted by date/time)
   - Click on booking card to see details
   - Can cancel booking (loses deposit + fee)
   - Can contact business/admin
   - Can rebook (redirects to business profile)
   - View finished bookings
   - View cancelled bookings

6. Business Profile View
   - Click on business name
   - View business profile
   - See: Story, Services, Staff
   - Filter services by date (calendar)
   - Click on service to book

BUSINESS FLOW
-------------
1. Account Creation
   - Create shopper account first
   - Request to become business
   - Wait for admin approval
   - Receive approval email
   - Business account activated

2. Business Setup
   - Upload logo
   - Add business details (name, email, phone, address)
   - Add bank details (BSB, account number)
   - Add staff members (name, photo, qualifications)
   - Add business story/description

3. Service Listing
   - Go to "List" tab
   - Select category (dropdown)
   - If sub-categories exist: select sub-category
   - Select service (from pre-programmed list)
   - Add date (calendar picker)
   - For each date:
     * Add time slots (start/end time)
     * Set cost per time slot
     * Assign staff members
   - Add multiple dates if needed
   - Select add-ons (from pre-programmed list)
   - Set add-on pricing
   - Add description
   - Set address (service location)
   - List service
   - Service appears in "List" tab (listed but not booked)

4. Booking Management
   - View "Bookings" tab
   - See upcoming bookings
   - Filter by date (calendar - next 7 days)
   - Filter by category/sub-category
   - Click on booking to see details
   - Can cancel booking (customer gets full deposit refund)
   - Can contact customer
   - After service: Mark as "Complete"
   - Booking moves to "Pending"
   - Enter bank details for payout
   - Submit → Booking moves to "Finished"
   - Receive payment (50% of deposit)

5. Staff Management
   - Go to "Staff" tab
   - View all staff members
   - Click on staff name to see details
   - Add new staff member
   - Edit staff information

6. Service Management
   - View "List" tab
   - See all listed but not booked services
   - Click on service to view/edit details
   - Can delete service
   - Can edit service details

ADMIN FLOW
----------
1. Business Approval
   - Review business requests
   - Approve or reject
   - If approved: Business account activated, email sent

2. Payment Processing
   - View admin panel
   - See completed bookings
   - See business bank details
   - Process payouts (50% of deposit)
   - Track payment status

BOOKING LIFECYCLE
-----------------
1. Created
   - Shopper completes payment
   - Booking status: "Upcoming"
   - Both parties receive confirmation email
   - Time slot blocked

2. Service Completed
   - Booking time passes OR end of day
   - Status: "Pending" (waiting for business to mark complete)
   - Business marks as "Complete"
   - Status: "Finished"
   - Business enters bank details
   - Payment processed (50% of deposit)

3. Cancellation
   - Shopper or Business cancels
   - Status: "Finished" with "Cancelled" label
   - Time slot becomes available
   - Refund processed (if applicable)
   - Both parties receive cancellation email

SEARCH & FILTER FLOW
--------------------
1. Click "Book" button on home page
2. Enter location (auto-suggest)
3. Select date (calendar picker)
4. Select category (dropdown)
5. If category has sub-categories: Select sub-category
6. Select service (dropdown)
7. View filtered results
8. Click on service to book

CART FLOW
---------
1. Add service to cart
2. Can add more services from same business
3. Cannot add services from different business
4. View cart with all services
5. See total cost breakdown
6. Proceed to checkout
7. Fill payment details
8. Complete payment
9. All services booked simultaneously
10. Cart cleared
