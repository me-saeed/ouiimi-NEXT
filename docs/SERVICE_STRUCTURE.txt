SERVICE DATA STRUCTURE
----------------------
Each Service Listing Contains:
- Category (required)
- Sub-Category (if applicable)
- Service Name (from pre-programmed list)
- Duration (in hours/minutes)
- Base Cost (set by business)
- Description (provided by business)
- Address (where service takes place)
- Multiple Dates (business can add)
- Multiple Time Slots per Date
  - Start Time
  - End Time
  - Cost (can vary per slot)
  - Staff Assignment (multiple staff per slot)
- Add-Ons (selected from pre-programmed list, custom pricing)
- Business Logo
- Business Name

DISPLAY PRIORITY
----------------
- Services shown by next closest available date/time
- Home page: First 6 cards per category
- Business profile: All services, filterable by date/category
- Search results: Filtered by location, date, category
